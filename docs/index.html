Why it's different

Your trading desk, with your crew on it.

No more sharing screenshots in a group chat. Hop on, talk, and watch each other trade the same charts live.

Live voice & video

Crystal-clear peer-to-peer audio and camera with your whole group, right in the chart tab.

Screen share

Show your setup, a tab, or your full screen. Pin anyone's share to the main stage.

Live chart presence

See exactly which symbol each person is viewing, updating in real time as they switch.

Get started

Install in under a minute.

It's not on the Chrome Web Store yet, so you load it manually today — three quick steps.

1

Download & unzip

Grab the extension and unzip it anywhere on your computer.

Download .zip ~20 KB
2

Load it in Chrome

Open chrome://extensions, turn on Developer mode, click Load unpacked, and pick the extension folder.

3

Open a chart

Head to a tradingview.com chart, join a room, and allow your mic — you're on the call.
